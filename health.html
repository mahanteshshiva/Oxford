Health care

  1. "A sound mind in a sound body": Keeping this in view, the school takes care of the health of each student. The school provides sports and games items for physical fitness of the child. Along with this, a team of highly experienced M.B.B.S. Doctors visit the school every year to examine and give proper health guidelines to students. The school also has tie-ups with well established hospitals to provide the students with the right health care. The school also has an infirmary with nursing and first-aid facility.
