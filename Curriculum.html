Curriculum


The syllabus for all the classes is as per the norms of C.B.S.E.. The medium of instruction is English and every student is expected to speak and converse in English only to improve his/her proficiency In English. The school uniquely promotes the integration of academic and co-curricular programmes, as well as the development of the informal curriculum where students are encouraged to build their personalities through learning and socio cultural interaction.
